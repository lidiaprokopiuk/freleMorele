My story

FreleMorele is located in San Francisco. This place is characterized by an intimate atmosphere and individual climate.

We bake crispy baguettes from which we prepare original sandwiches, also in the version warm from the oven. In addition to aromatic coffee, you can enjoy freshly squeezed juices and refreshing cocktails.

It doesn't matter if you're an early riser or an unreformable sleeper - we serve breakfast from morning to evening. If you come to us thinking of something to eat, we will offer a portion of fresh vegetable salad in various versions or hot cream soup. All this accompanied by our signature baguettes.

In the evening we offer an interesting selection of beers and wine. Served with seasonal snacks, they will ensure a pleasant time in an intimate atmosphere.
